Hi!

My name is Colin King.

I'm a junior at Rice University pursuing a B.S. in computer science and a business minor.

I'm currently working as a software engineering intern at CDK Global.

There are a few things I want people who visit this site to know about me. I study computer science and love software development, but "coding" is far from a passion of mine. I'm passionate about creating things, solving problems, and optimizing solutions. I'm in Houston now, but I've lived in Beijing, Hong Kong, and both coasts of the United States. I bring what I've learned from each place and the people there I've worked with to help me find creative ways to approach each problem I face. I've built applications that promote driver safety, satisfy picky palates at any restaurant, and even gamify the college student experience. Nothing gives me more joy than using what I've built to help others.
I'm excited about my future.
